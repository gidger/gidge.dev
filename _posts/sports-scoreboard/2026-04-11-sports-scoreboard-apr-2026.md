One more major Raspberry Pi LED matrix scoreboard update for the year, the MLB and WNBA (season's not canceled, woot) have been implemented! All expected scenes (live scores, favourite team's next game, and standings) are fully supported. Additionally, a new layout has been created for the MLB (or baseball in general, I guess), displaying the current inning, outs, and runners on base.

Here's a video of all the leagues implemented thus far, and some additional screenshots of the new functionality.
